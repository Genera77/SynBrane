About

SynBrane

SynBrane is an experimental sound engine designed to merge tuning theory, chord building, and rhythm generation into a single creative tool.

You sculpt chords using a spiraling circular interface tuned to any temperament — 12-EDO, 19-EDO, 24-EDO, 31-EDO, 8-EDO, or any Scala tuning — and hear them instantly as harmony or as rhythm.

Harmony Mode

In Harmony Mode, your chord plays as a synth sound:

  • Choose any temperament
  • Select notes on the spiral
  • Pick a root and chord preset
  • Shape the sound with ADSR, waveform, detune, and filter controls
  • Add arpeggiators per chord
  • Preview chords or render full loops from the backend

Harmony Mode acts like a microtonal chord workstation — but built around your tuning rather than a keyboard.

Rhythm Mode — Turning a chord into a beat

Rhythm Mode transforms your chord into a drum pattern.

Instead of playing pitches, each note you select becomes a percussive voice, and the chord’s structure becomes a timing model.

Here’s exactly how it works:

  • Each selected degree becomes a trigger point.
  • The degree is normalized to a fraction of one bar based on the tuning size.

The Rhythm × slider slows everything down.

  • 1.00× = normal timing
  • 0.30× = ~3× slower (default)
  • 0.10× = ~10× slower (deep, slow pulses)

This creates long, breathing rhythmic structures.

Each degree maps to a drum type.

  • Lower degrees → low drums
  • Mid degrees → claps / snares
  • High degrees → hats / ticks

Kick + Snare backbone stays locked.

Kick on beats 1/3, snare on 2/4.

The full chord becomes a rhythmic texture.

Every note fires at a slowed-down position in the bar, creating grooves that depend entirely on:

  • temperament structure
  • chord shape
  • rhythmic slowdown
  • tempo

Rhythm Mode essentially asks:

“What if your chord was your beat?”

Looping & Composition

  • Choose between 1 and 5 chords
  • Each chord has its own tuning, notes, root, preset, and arpeggiator
  • Loops repeat 10 times or until stopped
  • Preview the loop in-browser or render a WAV through the backend

SynBrane works like a microtonal groovebox for harmonic and rhythmic exploration.

Patch System

Save or load full patches as JSON containing:

  • Global synth settings
  • Tempo and rhythm multiplier
  • Loop chord count
  • All chord notes, tunings, roots, presets, and arpeggiators

Patches behave like portable “scenes” you can reload anytime.

Tuning Exploration

SynBrane supports:

  • 8-EDO
  • 12-EDO
  • 19-EDO
  • 22-EDO
  • 24-EDO
  • 31-EDO
  • Any Scala tuning

The spiral redraws for each system, showing you the structure of the tuning and giving you a direct way to hear the tuning’s interval relationships — both as harmony and as rhythm.
